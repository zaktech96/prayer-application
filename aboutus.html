What is Alwaqt

We are pleased to introduce Alwaqt, Alwaqt prayers app is a google chrome extension which displays prayer times depending on your location. Alwaqt translates roughly to time in Arabic. It also has a counter so you can keep track of how many times you have prayed. This project aims at creating a world where every Muslim can pray on time and be used by many Muslims around the world. The software will allow different screen sizes from mobile to desktop and bigger monitor screens which display prayer times depending on your location, so you can practice your prayers and stay connected with them. We look to create one of the best prayer applications as google chrome extensions, by using modern and dynamic designs.
